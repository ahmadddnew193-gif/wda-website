**2. Inconsistent Branding**
- Companies struggle to maintain a cohesive brand identity across platforms

- Building modern websites requires specialized skills

- Hiring full-time developers is expensive and often unnecessary

**The result?** Businesses lose customers to competitors with better digital experiences, miss out on revenue, and struggle to grow in the online marketplace.
